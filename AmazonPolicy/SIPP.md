# SIPP (Ships in Product Packaging)

A voluntary Amazon program that lets qualifying products ship in their own retail packaging — without an Amazon over-box. Stands for **Ships in Product Packaging**.

## How It Works

Without SIPP: Amazon adds its own over-box or mailer around your product's retail packaging.

With SIPP: Your product ships in its original manufacturer box. No Amazon over-box added.

## The 2026 Fee Trap

In 2026, Amazon restructured bulky item fees — base fees **decreased**. But only for SIPP-enrolled products.

```
Base fee savings (no SIPP):   ~$0.15–$0.30/unit
Mandatory packaging fee:      ~$1.50/unit  ← applies WITHOUT SIPP enrollment
Net effect:                   -$1.20 to -$1.35/unit WORSE without SIPP
```

For bulky items, NOT being in SIPP means you pay the reduced base fee **plus** a ~$1.50/unit mandatory packaging surcharge. The savings are entirely wiped out.

## SIPP Requirements

- **ISTA-6 durability standard** — Packaging must pass International Safe Transit Association 6-series testing. Validates that your packaging survives Amazon's handling without damage.
- **Enrolled per ASIN** — Not automatic. Must apply through Seller Central.
- **Original packaging** — Product must ship in its original manufacturer box. No poly-bagged or bundle units.

ISTA-6 testing is an upfront investment ($500–$2,000 per packaging type via certified labs). But for high-volume sellers moving 10,000+ bulky units/month, the annual savings ($180,000+) far exceed the testing cost.

## SIPP Savings by Size Tier

| Size Tier | Without SIPP | With SIPP | Savings/Unit |
|-----------|------------|----------|-------------|
| Small Bulky | Base fee − $1.50 penalty | Base fee only | ~$1.50 |
| Large Bulky | Base fee − $1.50 penalty | Base fee only | ~$1.50 |
| Standard (non-bulky) | ~$0.25–$1.32/unit savings vs over-box | | $0.25–$1.32 |

**Annual ROI example (Large Bulky, 10,000 units/month):**
```
Without SIPP: $1.50 × 10,000 × 12 = $180,000/year in penalties
With SIPP:    $0 additional cost
ISA-6 testing: ~$1,500 (one-time)
Payback period: < 1 month
```

## Related Concepts

- **[[FBA Prep]]** — Pre-shipment preparation (labeling, bundling). SIPP enrollment is separate from prep requirements.
- **ISTA-6** — International Safe Transit Association 6-series packaging durability standard. Required for SIPP. Tests drop, vibration, compression resistance.
- **Bulky Items** — In 2026, split into Small Bulky and Large Bulky (replaced "Large Bulky" tier).
